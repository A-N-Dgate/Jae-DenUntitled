This is a game based on the k-band Day6 

You play as former member Jae as he decides to visit his former
bandmates, but even after his planning something still goes wrong...
Use your detective skills to navigate your surroundings in text based
environment to find clues, and fight your way to find your friends.

This game is still in developement, and complete builds will be uploaded
to gamejolt, and test builds will be here.
